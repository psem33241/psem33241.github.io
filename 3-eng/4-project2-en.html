Toys & Models : SQL & BI Project

November 2024 - January 2025

Welcome to the Toys & Models project, part of my data analyst training at Wild Code School. We were commissioned by a company specializing in the sale of models to explore their existing database and develop an interactive, dynamic dashboard. This dashboard, updated daily, was designed to help the director manage the company efficiently.

Objective & Challenges

Our dashboard is structured around four main areas: sales, finance, logistics and human resources. Using key performance indicators (KPIs), we were able to provide valuable insights for each area:

  • Human Ressources : Analysis of sales representative and office performance.
  • Sales : Tracking sales, identifying top and flop sellers, and analyzing trends.
  • Finances : Evaluation of the most and least profitable customers and analysis of payment terms.
  • Logistics : Inventory monitoring, efficient order processing, and identification of late deliveries.

Tools & Resources

For this project, we used a SQL database accessible via a cloud server or local installation. We also used BI tools such as Power BI and Tableau to create a visual and accessible dashboard.

Deliverable

The final deliverable includes a presentation of the dashboard, demonstrating how KPIs can be used to manage and improve the company's performance in its various areas. We have also discussed the challenges encountered and the prospects for continuous improvement.
